iOS Application for Local Music Recommendation and Classification

I am currently developing an iPhone application for discovering local music events based on a users' music preferences, specifically focusing on artists that have not been classified using collaborative filtering methods. I am using Apple's Objective-C development tools, Flask for Python, and SQLite3.

Collages

In summer of 2012, while interning in Seattle, I participated in Facebook's Seattle summer hackathon. Together, with my partner Cassie, we developed a Facebook Application that creates collages out of a set of 3-5 photos and automatically posts them to your Facebook and places them in an album. We placed first in the hackathon and won some awesome JamBox speakers! We built it using Flask, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Check it out here!

KudoCast

This is a project I worked on at the Seattle Women's Startup Weekend in 2012. We developed a website for micro-recommendations for professionals. Simply put, KudoCast is to LinkedIn as Twitter is to Facebook. Users can receive "kudos" for small achievements at work, and take those with them to showcase during their next job search. This was developed using Jade, CSS, Node.js, and MongoDB. Check it out here!
